Panning and Zooming

Navigate the node graph canvas easily:

  • Pan (Move View): Hold the Middle Mouse Button and drag the mouse. The cursor changes to a closed hand while panning.
  • Zoom: Use the Mouse Scroll Wheel to zoom in and out, centered on the mouse cursor position.
  • Frame View (F): Press F to frame the entire graph (if nothing is selected) or the currently selected nodes within the view.

(Handled by NavigationManager)
