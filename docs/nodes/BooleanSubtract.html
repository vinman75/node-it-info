BooleanSubtract

Category: Geometry

Description: Performs a boolean subtraction: Shape B - Shape A. Inputs 'Shape A' and 'Shape B' should be closed polygons (List[[x,y]]) or lists of such polygons. Outputs the resulting geometry as a list of polygons. Requires 'shapely' library.

Inputs:

  • Shape A: list
  • Shape B: list

Outputs:

  • Output: list
