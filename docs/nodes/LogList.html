LogList

Category: List_Operators

Description: Takes a list ('List In') and applies the natural logarithm (math.log) to each positive numeric element or vector component. Handles simple lists [value,...], lists of lists/tuples [[x,y],...], and passes through dictionaries. Outputs a new list with results. Non-positive numbers or non-numeric items are passed through unchanged.

Inputs:

  • List In: list

Outputs:

  • Output: list
