NewtonForce

Category: Dynamics

Description: Simulates an acceleration field based on a 1/r^2 falloff towards/away from a point (like point gravity or electrostatic field). Magnitude controls the strength of the central source. The resulting acceleration applied by the Physics Solver is independent of the affected particle's mass.

Inputs:

  • Position: vector3
  • Magnitude: float
  • Min Distance: float
  • Max Distance: float
  • Attenuation: float

Outputs:

  • Output: dict
