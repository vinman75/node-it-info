Triangle

Category: Geometry

Description: Creates a path representing a triangle defined by its three vertex points ('Point A', 'Point B', 'Point C'). Outputs a list of [x, y] corner points.

Inputs:

  • Point A: vector2
  • Point B: vector2
  • Point C: vector2

Outputs:

  • Output: list
